# Torque

**Digital vehicle inspections that build customer trust — not just checklists.**

## The Problem

Auto shop inspections are still paper-based and opaque — customers get a bill, not proof. That gap drives declined services, disputed charges, and low trust, even when the work is legit.

## What Torque Does

Torque gives repair shops a digital inspection workflow that builds a transparent "trust report" for every customer — complete with photos, technician notes, and itemized findings — and lets customers approve repairs instantly via SMS. No app download, no phone calls, no guesswork.
- (Phase 1) 👇
- 📋 Digital inspection checklist for technicians
- 📸 Photo/video evidence attached to each finding
- 🔗 Shareable customer trust report
- ✅ SMS-based approval flow for faster sign-off.
 
- 💳 (Phase 2) Integrated payments
  
- 🤖 (Phase 3) AI-powered upsell suggestions
